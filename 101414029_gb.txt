George Brown College, established in 1967, is a public college located in Toronto, Canada. It offers a wide range of programs including diplomas, degrees, and certificates in fields such as business, health sciences, community services, culinary arts, and technology. The college emphasizes experiential learning and industry-relevant education.

There are 3 campuses, including: St. James Campus, Waterfront Campus and Casa Loma Campus.

There are 7 centres:
1. Arts, Design & Information technology
2. Business
3. Community Services & Early Childhood
4. Construction & Engineering Technologies
5. Health Sciences
6. Hospitality & Culinary Arts
7. Preparatory & Liberal Studies
